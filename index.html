The Sons of the Empire

A mesmerizing and complex fantasy saga. Sons of Empire tells the story of Trastan Mage and his friend Finn Raymos, who find their fates inexplicably manipulated and intertwined by the mysterious Council of Five. Mage aspires to follow in his fathers footsteps in the Mage Guild as the High Magister of Kardith Bath.

Biography

John Roberts

John Roberts, born in 1975 in Willowbrook, emerged as a celebrated writer with a passion for storytelling nurtured from a young age. After studying English Literature, he developed a distinctive voice blending magical realism and introspection. His debut novel, "Whispers of Eternity" (2003), earned widespread acclaim, marking the beginning of a diverse literary journey exploring themes from existentialism to social justice. Roberts has received numerous awards, including the Willowbrook Literary Award for Fiction in 2010. Beyond his writing, he is known for philanthropy, supporting initiatives for literacy. His impact on the literary world ensures a lasting legacy, with his works translated globally and cherished by readers.
